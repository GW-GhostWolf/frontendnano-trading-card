Gray Wolf

The reintroduction of gray wolves, a keystone species, into Yellowstone National Park has completely revitalized the Yellowstone ecosystem.

  • Scientific Name: Canis Lupus
  • Average Length: 6 feet
  • Average Height: 30 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 7 years
  • Average Weight: 100 pounds

Ranging in color from black to white, the gray wolf is a close relative of domesticated dogs. They live in extemely loyal, social, family packs consisting of a mated pair and their offspring with occastional acceptance of a disperser from another pack.
